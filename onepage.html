Can women compete with men in running?

Join us in an exploration of running data from the Finse Piste in Leuven to uncover both differences and similarities between men and women in amateur running and find out whether a fair competition between the sexes is possible.

Running equality between genders?

Gender distinctions in society are disappearing left and right. However, the distinction remains in sports, especially in running. Of course, there are physical differences, but are there other factors which contribute to their difference in performance? And can we shorten the gap one way or the other? We use data from the running app Strava to find out.

Where does this difference in performance come from?

Physical differences

Problem solved?

Men and women have the same interests in running and share the same habits. Still, men outperform women as a result of physical and other differences. Though we propose some solutions to close the gap, it remains an open problem with no ideal solution.
